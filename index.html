What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System(IELTS) is an international standardised test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. It is jointly managed by the British Council, and was established in 1989. IELTS is one of the major English-language tests in the world. IELTS is accepted by most Canadian academic institutions, including Douglas College.

About DOUGLASS COLLEGE*

It is the largest degree-granting college in British Columbia, Canada, educating close to 25,000 students per year.
Douglas offers the most bachelor's degrees and post-degree programs of any college in B.C., and is noted for combining the academic foundations of a university with the career-ready skills of a college.
Each year, close to 17,000 students (including 4,210 international students from over 92 countries) take for-credit courses at Douglas.
Over 960 experienced, teaching-focused faculty bring both academic rigor and real-world experience to the classroom at Douglas. Outside the classroom, over 900 other employees work together to create a friendly, inspiring, supportive and respectful environment.
