How to get useful information for preparing?

If you are ready to study yourself, sites and resources mentioned below will definitely help you to be effectively prepared for IELTS. You will find all the most important and useful links in one post here.

Listening

  • esl-lab.com - on this site you will find a lot of audio of different complexity on a various topics in dialogue and monologue formats
  • ted.com- a vast resource with useful videos with transcripts for each video
  • manythings.org - a site that perfectly prepares for Listening, after each audio you can go through dictations and exercises. Podcasts are also available here.

Reading

  • inspirational-short-stories.com - this site contains inspirational and fun stories of varying degrees of difficulty that will help improve your vocabulary.
  • bbc.com -the news all over the world in english.
  • economist.com - news and texts (not only on economic topics, but also many others).
  • nationalgeographic.com - texts about animals, plants, the environment, nature, travel and photography

Writing

  • ielts.org - IELTS official site
  • britishcouncil.org - this particular site could give a lot of advises to find right way for preparation
  • facebook.com-IELTSMOOC Group - a Facebook group for those who are preparing for IELTS and who are having trouble writing an essay. Here you can share the completed assignment in the preparation process and ask to check your assignment and indicate errors, as well as examine the quality of your letter for content and grammar.

Speaking

  • howtospell.co.uk - this site will help you to get the correct pronunciation of English words. Also here you can find interesting exercises and even games
  • bbc.co.uk - a great website for pronunciation training, after just a few lessons you will feel the result and learn to speak correctly
  • www.englisch-hilfen.de -a very useful online resource for working on grammar enhance and vocabulary.
