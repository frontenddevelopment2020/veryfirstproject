How to get a high score?

An important aspect about IELTS is the exam cannot be passed or not passed. It uses a system for assessing language proficiency on a nine-point scale. Thus, by writing an exam even for the lowest score, a person still receives a certificate.However, the IELTS certification does not guarantee admission to the university or a college. You can find 5 useful tips for each IELTS Section below. It will help you to be confident and awared on the exam.

    • Small spelling errors can greatly reduce the overall score of the entire 1 part.
    • Simultaneous listening to audio recordings, reading and writing is complicated, but it is possible - you need to practice a lot.
    • Do not panic if the topic seemed too complicated or the sound was not very clear. Be focused.
    • Do not leave spaces in the answer form - if you not sure, try to guess.
    • Listen carefully to the instructions, it might help you to get the idea of the task.
    • No additional time is allocated to fill out the forms with the correct answers. Fill it out during the answering.
    • Do not spend a lot of time on a question, the answer to which you do not know. Complete the following tasks. You can return to the unresolved issue at the end.
    • Do not focus on individual words and phrases that you cannot translate. This is time-consuming.
    • Check the spelling of the answers before entering them on the form.
    • Before exams, read as many different books in English as possible: magazines, newspapers, scientific articles. Do not limit your horizons to one type of literature.
    • Leave a few minutes to check your essay.
    • Do not repeat the same idea twice.
    • Keep track of time. Perform all tasks at a strictly defined time.
    • Avoid informal phrases when writing a specific topic.
    • Learn to determine the volume of the text. Time is your enemy.
    • Try to speak more English than the examiner.
    • Ask the examiner for an explanation of the issue if you do not understand it.
    • Do not prepare in advance template phrases for answers to any questions. The examiner will understand and change questions and topics.
    • This part tests not so much grammatical as conversational communication skills in English.
    • Before the exam, speak any topics on the recorder. This will be the perfect preparation for you.
